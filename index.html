our mission

our focus lies in designing and implementing socioeconomic activities that drive productivity and growth for our clients. With our expertise in environment, mining, forestry, project management, policy, and research.

scope

Our main focus, in addition to our Environmental Expertise, lies in conducting socioeconomic surveys and analysis to provide valuable insights into the economic, social, and cultural aspects of communities.

policy & plan

Based on our surveys and analysis, we assist governments, NGOs, and other stakeholders in developing effective plans, policies, and projects.
